Routes:

Get index/home page (lists all to-dos):
GET ( “/”)

Get a registration page:
GET (“/users/new”)

Create a new user:
POST(“/users/new”)

Get new To Do creation page:
GET(“/users/:uid/todos/new”)

Create a new To Do:
POST (“/users/:uid/todos/new”)

Update a To Do:
PUT(“/users/:uid/todos/:tid”)

Delete a To Do:
DELETE(“/users/:uid/todos/:tid”)

Connect with API:
POST(“/users/:uid/todos/:tid”)
